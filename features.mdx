Paperguide offers a suite of advanced features designed to enhance your research productivity:

## Research Assistant

The AI-powered assistant helps you discover relevant materials and suggests articles based on your interests.

## Reference Manager

Easily organize and cite your sources. Export citations in multiple formats for seamless integration into your work.

## Writing Assistant

Enhance your writing with AI-powered suggestions and grammar checks. Collaborate with peers in real-time.

Explore these features to optimize your research workflow.
