Welcome to the **Paperguide** documentation. Paperguide is an AI-powered research platform that streamlines the research experience for students, researchers, and academics. With tools like a research assistant, reference manager, and writing assistant, Paperguide enhances productivity and uncovers deeper insights.

## Why Paperguide?

Paperguide offers a modern, intuitive interface and is available in multiple languages. Its features include:

- **Smart Discovery**: Find relevant research materials quickly.
- **Intelligent Analysis**: Get in-depth insights and analytics.
- **Streamlined Writing**: Use advanced writing tools to simplify document creation.
