## Setting Up Your Account

1. **Sign Up**: Visit [Paperguide](https://paperguide.com) and create an account.
2. **Verify Email**: Confirm your email address through the verification link.
3. **Login**: Access your dashboard to start your research journey.

## Navigating the Dashboard

The dashboard provides a central hub for all your research activities:

- **Research Assistant**: Discover articles and papers.
- **Reference Manager**: Organize and cite your sources.
- **Writing Assistant**: Craft and edit your documents efficiently.

Explore these features to make the most of Paperguide.
