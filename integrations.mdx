Paperguide seamlessly integrates with various platforms to streamline your research process:

## Supported Platforms

- **Google Scholar**: Import articles and references directly.
- **Mendeley**: Sync your library for comprehensive management.
- **Microsoft Word**: Use the Paperguide plugin to insert citations and manage references.

## Setting Up Integrations

1. **Access Integrations**: Go to the integrations section in your dashboard.
2. **Authorize Connections**: Follow prompts to authorize connections with your accounts.

Integrate Paperguide with your favorite tools to enhance your research experience.
